00022  * Classic recursive descent parser.
00023  * Builds a RPN representation in _list.
00024  * Numbers stay as strings.
00025  *
00026  * BNF:
00027  * arglisttail  : "," expression arglisttail
00028  *              | nothing
00029  * arglist      : "(" expression arglisttail ")";

00031  *              | nothing
00032  * leaf         : name arglist
00033  * factor       : unary "(" expression ")"
00034  *              | unary leaf
00035  * termtail     : "*" factor termtail
00036  *              | "/" factor termtail
00037  *              | nothing
00038  * term         : factor termtail
00039  * addexptail   : "+" term addexptail
00040  *              | "-" term addexptail
00041  *              | nothing
00042  * addexp       : term addexptail
00043  * logicaltail  : "<" addexp logicaltail
00044  *              | ">" addexp logicaltail
00045  *              | "<=" addexp logicaltail
00046  *              | ">=" addexp logicaltail
00047  *              | "==" addexp logicaltail
00048  *              | "!=" addexp logicaltail
00049  *              | nothing
00050  * logical      : addexp logicaltail
00051  * andtail      : "&&" logical andtail
00052  *              | nothing
00053  * andarg       : logical andtail
00054  * exptail      : "||" andarg exptail
00055  *              | nothing
